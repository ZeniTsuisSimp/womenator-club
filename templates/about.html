Our Story

Who We Are

Womenovators Club is a vibrant college community dedicated to empowering women in STEM fields.

Founded with the vision of bridging the gender gap in technology, we bring together passionate women who aspire to innovate, lead, and create impact through technology.

Mission

Empower women through technology education, hands-on experience, and community support.

Vision

A world where every woman has equal opportunity to thrive in technology and innovation.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Inclusivity

We welcome everyone regardless of background, experience, or skill level.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and building real-world solutions.

Community

We believe in the power of collaboration and supporting each other.
